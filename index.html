How to Play

Desktop Controls:

  • ← → : Move piece left/right
  • ↓ : Move piece down
  • ↑ : Rotate piece

Mobile Controls:

  • Use on-screen buttons
  • Tap ↻ to rotate
  • Tap arrows to move

Game Rules:

  • Complete lines to score points
  • Stack reaches top = Game Over
  • 100 points per line cleared
